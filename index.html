Constitution Highlights

Identity
Name: Bangladesh Youth Community Verona. Estd: 30 July 2024. Office: Verona City.
Principles
To increase brotherhood, perform social welfare, preserve culture, and dignify the community.
Constitution
To establish strong social bonds and a sense of brotherhood among all Bangladeshi youth living in Verona, Italy, regardless of religion, caste, tribe, and political ideology.
